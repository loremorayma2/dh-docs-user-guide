### 💡 Tips

* Choose between full-screen or window recording. You can also include your voice or webcam for clearer communication.

* Use screen recording when a static image can’t explain a process clearly.

* Keep your Byte short (under 2 minutes) for easier sharing and feedback.

* Record only the window you need to keep the focus clean.

* After recording, check the preview before sharing to make sure everything is visible.
