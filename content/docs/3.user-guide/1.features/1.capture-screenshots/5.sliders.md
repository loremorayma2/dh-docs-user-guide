Creating a slider is easy — just click “New Slider”, give it a title, and start adding your content. You can rearrange slides, edit them anytime, and share them with your teammates.

This is perfect for collaborating with your team — everyone can view, comment, and contribute in one shared space. It keeps all your ideas, feedback, and visuals together, helping your team stay connected and creative.

💡 Tips for Using Sliders

🧩 Keep it simple: Use short text and clear visuals to make each slide easy to follow.

🤝 Collaborate freely: Invite your teammates to add comments or ideas directly on your slides.

🎨 Be creative: Try different layouts or colors to make your presentation stand out.

📢 Share with confidence: Present or share your slider anytime — everything stays organized in one place.
