💡 Tips

* Use sharing links to collect feedback quickly.

* Choose who can access your dot, keep it private, team-only, or public depending on your needs.

* Add annotations or text boxes before sharing to make your message even clearer.

* During presentations, share your screen or dot to explain your ideas live.
