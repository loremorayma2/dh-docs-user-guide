💡 Tips

* Use emojis to react to comments — for example: 👍 for agreement, ❤️ for appreciation, 😂 for something funny.

* Combine emojis with annotations to draw attention to important details.

* Don’t overuse them — choose 1–2 per dot to keep the message clear.

* Try using consistent emoji colors or styles across your team for easy recognition.
