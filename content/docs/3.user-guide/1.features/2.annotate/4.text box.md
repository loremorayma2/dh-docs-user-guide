💡 Tips

* Use the Text Box to share your thoughts or guide your team with short, clear messages.

* Start with the pre-written text and adjust it to fit your context. Tt’s fast and flexible!

* Combine Text Boxes with Annotations, Emojis, or Stickers to make your dots more visual and fun.

* Keep it short and sweet — a few sentences are enough to get your point across.
