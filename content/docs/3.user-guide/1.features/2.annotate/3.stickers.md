💡 Tips

* Use stickers to show reactions or acknowledge comments quickly — for example: ✅ Approved, ⚠️ Needs attention, 🌟 Great idea!

* Combine stickers with annotations or emojis to reinforce your message visually.

* Keep stickers minimal to avoid cluttering your dot — 1–3 per screenshot is usually enough.

* Use stickers consistently across your team to make feedback quickly recognizable.
