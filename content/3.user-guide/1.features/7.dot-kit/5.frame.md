### 📝 How to use Frames

🧾 Make sure you have a dot ready that you want to style.

🔄 Click the “Rotate Frame” button in the toolbar.

🔍 Each click cycles through the available frame styles.

📍Keep rotating until you find the frame that best fits your dot and context.

💾 Once you have the desired frame, your dot is ready to share, present, or review.

### 💡Tips

* Use frames to visually differentiate dots for presentations, feedback, or reviews.

* Stick to consistent frame styles within the same project for a clean and professional look.

* Combine frames with annotations, highlights, or stickers to make key information stand out.
