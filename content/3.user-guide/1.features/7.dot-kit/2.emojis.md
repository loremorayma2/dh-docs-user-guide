### 📝 How to use Emojis

🧾 Make sure you’re inside the Dot where you want to add an emoji.

😀 Click the Emoji icon on the toolbar to open the emoji menu.

🔍 Scroll through categories or use the search bar to quickly find the emoji you want.

😊 Click the emoji to place it directly inside your Dot, exactly where your cursor is positioned.

🧾 Drag the emoji to reposition it if you're adding it on top of a dot.

⚡ Move, change, or delete your emoji if you need to clarify your annotation.

💡 Tips

* Use emojis to react to comments — for example: 👍 for agreement, ❤️ for appreciation, 😂 for something funny.

* Combine emojis with annotations to draw attention to important details.

* Don’t overuse them — choose 1–2 per dot to keep the message clear.
