With the Add Sliders feature, you can quickly create a slider deck by adding, removing, or rearranging slides — all inside your dot workspace. Each slide can hold its own visuals, notes, and annotations, making your explanation clearer and more structured. 

### 📝  How to Add a Slider

#### Method 1: Upload Files

📂 Navigate to the dot where you want to build your slider.

⬆️ Select the Upload icon to add files from your device.

📥 Each uploaded file becomes its own slide automatically.

🔧 Add annotations, rearrange slides, or rename the slider as needed.

#### Method 2: Capture New Slides

🖥️ Prepare the content you want to snapshot.

🎥 Or Click the Capture button and use any of the keyboard shortcuts for faster workflow:

* Alt + S → Capture a selected area

* Alt + A → Capture the entire screen

* Alt + C → Capture a specific component 

⚡ Each capture instantly creates a new slide inside your slider.

✏️ Add notes, shapes, highlights, or comments to each captured slide.

### 💡 Tips

* Use uploads for polished visuals, and captures for real-time walkthroughs.

* Keep slides concise — one idea or step per slide improves clarity.

* Mix both methods if needed: upload designed assets and capture real screens.

* Reorder slides to adjust the flow of your explanation or presentation.

* Name your slider clearly to make it easy to locate and share later.
