### 📝 How to Use the Dot Browser

#### 1. 🗂️ Access the Dot Browser from the Panel

* Open the DOT App
* Click the Dot Browser icon (folder symbol) located in the main panel.
* You will see a complete lists of all your created dots, from oldest to newest.

#### 2. 📄 Explore Dot Browser Complete

* Once inside the Dot Browser, you will find several sections and tools:

##### 📚 Doc Lists

* In the All Documents section, you can view every type of document supported by the platform.

#### 🧭 Document Tools

Inside the document toolbar, you can use:

##### * ⬆️ Upload
Upload files directly from your computer.

##### * 🕒 Recent

View only the most recently added or edited documents.

##### * 🔗 Related

See documents related to the dot or context you’re working in.

##### * 📁 All

View all documents created, sorted from newest to oldest.

#### 3. 🖥️ Expand the Dot Browser

* Click the Expand icon next to the Exit button.

* The Dot Browser switches into full-screen mode, giving you a broader view of your dots and documents.

* This mode is ideal for reviewing long lists or working visually.

#### 4. 🔍 Additional Tools Inside the Dot Browser

##### * 🔎 Search Bar
Quickly find any dot by typing keywords or titles.

##### * ⚙️ Filter Button
Filter documents by type (images, PDFs, text files, etc.).

##### * 🖼️ Slide View Mode
View all your dots as horizontal slide cards, and navigate from left to right like a gallery.

### 💡 Tips

* Use filters to reduce visual clutter when working with many documents.

* Switch to full-screen mode for easier browsing and dot comparison.

* Upload files from Dot Browser when organizing large collections of documents.
