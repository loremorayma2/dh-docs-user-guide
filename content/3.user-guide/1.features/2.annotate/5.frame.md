### 💡Tips

* Choose a frame style that matches the purpose of your Dot. Subtle for notes.

* Use frames to create visual consistency across your workspace.

* Combine them with annotations or stickers to make specific details pop.

* Remember: frames are for style and focus, not structure — keep them simple and clean.
