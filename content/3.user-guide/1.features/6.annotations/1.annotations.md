Double-click anywhere on the screen to open a floating text box where you can instantly jot down your thoughts. It helps capture ideas quickly and clearly without interrupting your workflow.

### 💡 Tips 

* Combine tools for the best results — for example, use the Pen for shapes, the Highlighter for emphasis, and the Magic Marker for quick comments.

* Keep your color palette consistent so your annotations look neat and easy to read.

* Remember, you can switch between tools by clicking the annotation icon and selecting with a left click.
