### 📝 How to use Shape Annotations

🧾 Click the Shapes Annotation icon to access the drawing tools.

🔶 Select a preset shape (circle, rectangle, arrow)

🖊️ Or Right-click to enable the pen and draw your shape.

⚡ Click on your shape to move, resize, change stroke, or remove the shape if you need to refine it.

💬 Once your drawing is placed, hover over it and Start a Thread to begin a conversation directly linked to your shape.

### 💡 Tips 

* Use shapes to highlight important elements or draw attention to key areas in a screenshot.

* Combine annotations with Threads to start conversations directly about the highlighted area.

* Keep annotations simple and clean to avoid clutter and maintain clarity in your Dot.
