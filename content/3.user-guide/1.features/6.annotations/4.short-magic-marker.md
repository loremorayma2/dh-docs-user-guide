### 📝 How to use Short Magic Marker

🧾 Make sure you are inside the Dot where you want to place a short marker.

✴️ Double-click on the area where you want to add your short marker to open the Magic Marker bar.

✏️ Type a brief word, label, or phrase that you want to highlight inside the Dot.

🎨 Change the background color of your marker to make it stand out or categorize information visually.

🔣 Attach an icon from the toolbar to represent the meaning or category of your marker.

### 💡 Tips 

* Keep Magic Marker notes short—ideal for labels, categories, or quick reminders.

* Use background colors to create visual grouping or to emphasize urgency or type of note.

* Adding icons helps teammates immediately understand the context at a glance.
