### 📝 How to use Free Drawing

🧾 Click the Shapes Annotation icon to access the drawing tools.

✏️ Right-click to enable free drawing.

⚡ Click and drag on your Dot to sketch your custom outline, circle, or marking.

⚡ Move, refine, or delete your drawing if you need to clarify your annotation.

💬 Once your drawing is placed, hover over it and Start a Thread to begin a conversation directly linked to your drawing.

### 💡 Tips 

* Free Drawing is ideal for quick sketches, circling items, or adding loose visual notes.

* Keep your free drawings minimal to avoid clutter and maintain clarity in the Dot.

* Use Free Drawing for flexible annotations and Shapes for more structured ones.
