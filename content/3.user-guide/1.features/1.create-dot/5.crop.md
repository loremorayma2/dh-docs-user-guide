### 🟠 Click and Drag

Opens the quick capture bar. Select the option of capture. In this case the third button.

You can even adjust the selection afterward to make it fit perfectly.

It’s a simple way to focus on what matters, share clear feedback, or highlight important details—making collaboration fast and easy.

You can also use ALT + C for a quicker way to do it.

### 💡 Tips

* Use the shortcut by Pressing ALT + C to quickly start a Click and Drag capture without interrupting your workflow.

* Click and drag to capture only the section you want, keeping your Dot clean and focused.

* After capturing, resize or move the selection to make sure it perfectly fits the area you need.

* Capture only what’s necessary to make your Dot clear and easy to understand.
