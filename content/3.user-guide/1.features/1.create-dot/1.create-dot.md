### 📝 How to Take a Screenshot

📥 Open the quick capture bar. Make your cursor to the end of the page anytime to capture.  

📸 You can also create a Dot by taking a quick screenshot directly from your workspace.

You can use ALT + S for a quicker way to do it.

### 💡 Tips

* Each capture automatically becomes a Dot, ready to annotate, organize, and share with your team.

* Use keyboard shortcuts, Pressing ALT + S for a fast and seamless way to create a new Dot without interrupting your workflow.

* Before taking a screenshot, hover over the exact area you want to highlight so your Dot stays clean and focused.
