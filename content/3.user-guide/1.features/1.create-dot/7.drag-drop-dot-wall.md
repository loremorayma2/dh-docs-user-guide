Grab a file from your desktop or any folder and drop it directly onto your Dot wall.

As soon as you release the file, DOT will automatically create a new Dot for you.

It’s the fastest way to bring your visuals into DOT without opening the capture bar—just drag, drop, and you're ready to collaborate.

### 💡 Tips

* Ideal when you want to skip the capture bar and add visuals in seconds.

* Make sure the file you’re dragging is the one you want to share or annotate.

* Works great with images, screenshots, and documents. Whatever you need to collaborate smoothly.
