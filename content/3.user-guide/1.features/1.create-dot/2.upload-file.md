### 💡 Tips

* Supported formats include .png, .jpg, and .mp4. Keep all your visuals in one place for easy access.

* Drag and drop your file directly into the workspace — no need to browse manually.

* Use descriptive file names before uploading to keep your library tidy.

* You can upload multiple files at once and organize them later as separate Dots.

* Combine uploaded images with new captures to document progress or design iterations.
