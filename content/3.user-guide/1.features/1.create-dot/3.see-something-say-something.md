### 🔵 Click and Highlight 

Opens the quick capture bar. Select the option of capture. In this case the second button.

Highlight a specific part of your screen by clicking right and using the highlight pen.

It’s ideal for capturing a specific detail or section you want to share with your team.

You can also use ALT + A for a quicker way to do it.

### 💡 Tips

* Use the quick access shortcut by Pressing ALT + A to open the capture tool instantly and highlight what matters without breaking your workflow.

* Highlight only the important areas of your screen to keep your message clear and easy to understand.

* Right-click and drag with the highlight pen to mark the exact section you want to emphasize.

* Add a brief note or comment after capturing to provide context and avoid confusion.
