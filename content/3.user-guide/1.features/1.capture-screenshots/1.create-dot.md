### 📸 Capture Screenshots

You can create a Dot by taking a quick screenshot directly from your workspace.
There are three simple ways to capture your screen — choose the one that best fits your workflow.

### 🟢 1. Hover to the bottom of the page

Opens the quick capture bar. Make your cursor to the end of the page anytime to capture. You can also use ALT + S.

### 🔵 2. Click and Drag

Select a specific part of your screen. Ideal for capturing a detail or a section, you can drag with the pen tool to mark a specific section on the page. You can also use ALT + A.

  <div class="border rounded-lg shadow-md mt-4 h-60">

### 🟠3. Click and Drag

Capture content inside a chosen window or component. Great for focusing on one tool or app area. You can also use ALT + C.

### 💡 Tips

* Each capture automatically becomes a Dot, ready to annotate, organize, and share with your team.

* Use Alt + S for full-page captures — it’s the fastest way to grab everything in view.

* Try Alt + A when you only need to show one specific element or bug.

* After taking a screenshot, add a short caption before you forget the context.
