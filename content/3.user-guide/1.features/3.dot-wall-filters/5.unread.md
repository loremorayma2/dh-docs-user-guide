### Step-by-Step

🧾 Click the Unread tab to filter messages that haven’t been read.

📄 Browse the messages marked as unread and check the important ones first.

✅ After reviewing: 

- Click on the three points.
 
- Mark as Read to update their status and remove them from the Unread filter.

### 💡Tips

* Unread messages are automatically tracked, so you never have to manually check which ones are new.

* Combine with Pinned Dots to prioritize and monitor critical messages efficiently.

* Check the Unread section regularly to stay up-to-date and prevent important messages from being overlooked.
