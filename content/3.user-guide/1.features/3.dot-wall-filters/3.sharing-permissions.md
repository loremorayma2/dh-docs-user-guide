## ✨ Here's what you can do?

- Choose if your Dot is private, public, or shared only with specific team members.

- Share access by mentioning collaborators you want to work with.

- Set different permission levels — allow others to edit, view, or comment only.

- Stop sharing or update permissions anytime — you’re always in control.



💡 Tip: Before sharing, check your permissions to make sure each collaborator has the right level of access. You can easily change it later if needed!
