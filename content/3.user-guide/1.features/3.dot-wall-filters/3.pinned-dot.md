It’s perfect for highlighting key information, urgent tasks, or anything you want to keep front and center. Stay organized and focused by keeping your most relevant dots pinned and easy to find.

### How to Use

🧾 Select a Dot — Choose the Dot you want to prioritize.

📌 Click the three points and pin the Dot — This will pin the Dot to the top of your list.

🧾 Unpin if Needed 

* Click the pin again, 
* Go to the three points 
* Click in unpin to remove it from the top.

### 💡Tips

* You can pin multiple Dots to keep all your important information visible.

* Use pinned Dots for urgent tasks, key notes, or anything you want to reference quickly.
