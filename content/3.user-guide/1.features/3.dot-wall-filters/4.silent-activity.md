### Step-by-Step

##### 🧾 Access the Dot or Thread

- Navigate to the Dot or thread you want to monitor silently.

##### 🔕 Enable Silent Activity

- Click the Silent Activity button to activate silent tracking.

- The system will now log actions related to this Dot without sending notifications.

##### 🧾 View Silent Activity Logs

- Open the activity log to see all updates recorded silently.

##### 🔕 Disable Silent Activity 

- Click the icon again to resume normal notifications.

### 💡Tips

* Use Silent Activity for drafts, minor edits, or testing, so teammates aren’t interrupted unnecessarily.

* Combine with Pinned Dots to silently monitor important messages or tasks.

* Silent Activity logs can be reviewed at any time to track progress discreetly.
