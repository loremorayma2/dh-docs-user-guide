### 💡Tips

1. in what matters most: Use pinned dots for tasks, notes, or ideas that you need to access quickly.

2. Keep your space clean: Unpin dots once they’re no longer a priority to avoid clutter.

3. Use it as a reminder: Pin urgent or time-sensitive dots so they stay in your line of sight.

4. Review regularly: Check your pinned dots from time to time to make sure they still match your current priorities.
