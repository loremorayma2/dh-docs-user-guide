### 📝 How to use Nofies

🔔 Click the Nofies button to view all notifications related to your activity.

📬 Browse through the list to see new mentions, replies, updates, or interactions where you were involved.

👆 Selecting any notification will take you directly to the exact dot and comment thread where the action happened.

📍 After reviewing a notification, it automatically marks as “read,” helping you keep track of what’s pending.

### 💡 Tips 

* Use Nofies to quickly catch up after being away — no need to search through dots manually.

* Check your unread Nofies first to prioritize what requires action.




New Nofies appear in real time as your team interacts with your dots.
With Nofies, collaboration becomes smoother everyone stays informed in real time, without the need to check messages constantly.
