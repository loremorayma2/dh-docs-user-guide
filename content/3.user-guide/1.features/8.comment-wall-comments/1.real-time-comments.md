### 📝 How to use Real-Time Comments

🧾 Navigate to the dot where you want to add or view comments.

🖊️ Type your message in the comment box and press Enter to post it instantly.

👀 Any teammate's response will appear immediately, allowing smooth back-and-forth communication.

📌 Use @username to notify someone directly and draw attention to specific details.

💾 Comments remain attached to the dot for future reference and ongoing discussion.

### 💡 Tips

* Leave short, clear comments right where your feedback is needed.

* Add emoji reactions (👍 ❤️ 😄) to make your replies more fun and expressive.

* Combine comments with annotations or highlights to give more precise context.

* Use @mentions to notify the right people and avoid communication delays.
