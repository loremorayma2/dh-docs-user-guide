### 📝 How to use Reactions

🧾 Navigate to the dot or thread where you want to react to a comment or message.

😀 Move your cursor over the comment or message you want to react to.

🖱️ A small emoji reaction button will appear — click it to open the reaction menu.

🎉 Choose from the reaction list (e.g., 👍, 😀, ❗, ❤️). Your reaction will appear instantly under the comment.

🔄 Click the same emoji again to remove it, or select another one to update your reaction.

👀 Hover over any reaction to see all users who reacted with that emoji.

### 💡 Tips

* Use 👍 to quickly acknowledge a message without adding noise to the thread.

* Use ❤️ to show appreciation or support.

* Use ❗ for messages requiring urgent attention.

* Reactions help reduce clutter — perfect for fast-moving teams or long threads.

* They’re great for confirming you’ve seen an update without needing a full comment.
