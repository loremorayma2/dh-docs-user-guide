## 🚀 About DOT 

Dot is a versatile platform that allows you to create, organize, and share visual notes, screenshots, and documents called Dots. It is designed to help individuals and teams stay organized, communicate effectively, and manage tasks or feedback in a central workspace.

Dot makes it easy to capture important information, annotate visuals, and collaborate seamlessly — all in one place, without losing track of anything.

---

### ⚙️ Why DOT exists

Managing ideas, visual feedback, and team collaboration can be time-consuming and scattered across multiple tools. Dot solves this problem by consolidating your workflow into a single, intuitive platform. It ensures that important information doesn’t get lost and makes collaboration faster, clearer, and more organized.

---

### 👥 Target Users

Dot is ideal for:

* **Teams and collaborators** who need to review, share, and provide feedback on visual content.

* **Individuals** looking to organize notes, screenshots, and important information efficiently.

* **Professionals** who want to streamline workflows, track progress, and maintain clear communication.

Whether you’re a designer, project manager, developer, or content creator, Dot adapts to your needs and helps you stay productive.

---

### 🎯 Purpose of This Manual

This User Guide will take you step by step through the process:

* Navigating your workspace.

* Capturing, organizing, and annotating dots.

* Collaborating and sharing efficiently.

* Using shortcuts, browser extensions, and advanced features.

### 📁 Scope of the Manual

This manual covers all essential aspects of Dot, including:

* Home
