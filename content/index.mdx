    # Frequently Asked Questions <strong class="text-pink-600">(FAQ)</strong> ❓
  ::
  ::div{class="flex flex-col justify-start items-start w-full"}
    ::accordion{default-value="first-item" collapsible class="flex w-full flex-col"}
      ::accordion-item{value="first-item"}
      #title
      ###### 1. What is DOT APP?

      #content
      **DOT APP** is a visual collaboration platform that allows you to capture screens, add annotations, and share ideas in real time, from your desktop or browser.
      ::

      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 2. Which operating systems are supported?

      #content
      - **Windows 10 or higher**  
      - **MacOS 11 or higher**  
      - Extensions available for **Chrome** and **Edge**
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 3. Can I work offline?

      #content
      Yes, you can capture and annotate images offline, but **real-time collaboration** requires an internet connection.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 4. How do I share my captures with the team?

      #content
      You can use the **collaboration link** from the Control Panel or automatically sync with your team if everyone has DOT APP installed.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 5. How do I create a new thread?

      #content
      Take a capture using the wall dot option in the main panel, write your message or comment, and select the members who will participate. Once created, all participants can contribute ideas and comments immediately.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 6. Can I work on multiple threads at the same time?

      #content
      Yes. You can have multiple active threads and switch between them from the sidebar. Each thread maintains its own history of messages and activity.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 7. How do I know who is connected and collaborating in a thread?

      #content
      At the top of the wall dot, you’ll see a list of users connected in real time. You’ll also see an activity indicator when someone is typing or making changes.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 8. Are changes saved automatically?

      #content
      Yes. All information shared in threads is saved automatically and in real time to prevent data loss.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 9. Can I mention colleagues inside a thread?

      #content
      Yes. Use the @ symbol followed by the username to notify them directly within the thread.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 10. Can I attach files or documents to a thread?

      #content
      Yes. You can drag and drop files into the thread or use the “Attach” button. All members with access can view and download the files.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 11. Who can see the threads I create?

      #content
      By default, only the users you invite will have access.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 12. I’m not seeing real-time updates. What should I do?

      #content
      Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, refresh the page or log out and log back in.
      ::
      ::accordion-item
      #title
      ###### 13. I forgot my password. How can I recover it?

      #content
      Click on “Forgot your password?” on the login screen and follow the steps to reset it using your registered email.
      ::
