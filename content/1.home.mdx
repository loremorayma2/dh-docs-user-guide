## DOT APP 🚀 – Visual Collaboration for Teams

**DOT APP** provides automated generative dots making the images insightful and RUMA **(Reusable, Understandable, Modular, Accurate)**, this is designed for asynchronous communication and collaboration through structured message dots. It allows users to create, manage and respond to dots based discussions across devices.
